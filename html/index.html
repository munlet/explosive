from : Honkai Star rail
my beloved wife

Acheron, real name Raiden Bosenmori Mei (Japanese: 雷電忘川守芽衣), is a playable character in Honkai: Star Rail. A drifter claiming to be a Galaxy Ranger, though she is later carrying with her a long sword. Though aloof and taciturn, her blade flicks out like lashing lightning. And yet, she always strikes with her scabbard, never drawing the sword free.




"Lone voyagers in the cosmos are driven by two desires — to tread in the trails of the past and to forge their own way. But under THEIR scrutiny... most end up adhering to the former."
